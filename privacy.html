PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Extra Ball Studios ("we", "us", "our") operates the Bad Thumbs mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

DATA WE COLLECT

Bad Thumbs does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. All gameplay data (scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — We display ads through Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services — Required for app distribution and may collect device and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Games Services — Used to power leaderboard functionality. App interaction data is transmitted to Google to track scores and rankings. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — Used to process in-app purchases. Purchase history is collected and managed by Google to enable and restore purchases. We do not collect or store your payment information directly. See Google's Privacy Policy.

AD PERSONALIZATION

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

DATA RETENTION

Since we do not collect personal data directly, there is no data for us to retain or delete. Third-party services manage their own data retention per their respective policies.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

CONTACT US

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at extraballstudios@gmail.com.
